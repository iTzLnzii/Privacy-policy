Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information is handled when you use this mobile game (“the Game”).

Last Updated: February 2026 Contact: dalenziidev@gmail.com

1) Introduction

This Privacy Policy is provided by Dalenzii Dev (“we”, “us”, “our”). By using the Game, you agree to the data practices described here.

2) Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information (such as your name, phone number, or email) through the Game.

However, the Game uses third-party services (listed below) that may collect certain information automatically to provide ads, handle purchases, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.

3) Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays ads using Google AdMob (including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads). AdMob may collect and process information such as:

  • Device identifiers (for example, Advertising ID)
  • IP address (often used to infer approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction and performance data (impressions, clicks)
  • Diagnostics and anti-fraud signals
Personalized vs. non-personalized ads: Where required by law (for example in the EEA/UK), AdMob may request consent to serve personalized ads. If consent is not granted (or not required), AdMob may serve non-personalized ads where available.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4) In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by the platform store (e.g., Google Play). We do not receive or store your full payment details (such as credit card numbers).

5) Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers to store personal data from the Game. Third-party services (like AdMob and the app store) may retain data under their own policies and legal obligations.

6) Children and Age Eligibility

The Game is not directed specifically to children, but it is intended to be playable by a general audience (including teens and adults). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information to us directly, contact us at dalenziidev@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

Note: Third-party advertising and platform services may process certain identifiers automatically. If you plan to target children as the primary audience, you should configure your ad settings for child-directed treatment.

7) EEA/UK Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may have rights under GDPR, including access, deletion, objection, and withdrawal of consent for certain processing.

When applicable, consent preferences for advertising are presented in-game through a consent form provided by Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP).

8) California Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights to request information about data collected and to request deletion. Advertising-related processing is handled primarily by third parties (such as Google AdMob).

9) Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the Game and its integrations configured securely. No system is 100% secure, but we aim to minimize risk and use reputable providers.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page and reflected by the “Last Updated” date above.

11) Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: dalenziidev@gmail.com
