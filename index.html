1) Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the Game. By using the Game, you agree to the practices described here.

Scope note: This policy covers the Game and its built-in third-party services (ads and purchases). It does not cover third-party websites or services you may access from within ads.

2) Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email through the Game.

However, third-party services integrated into the Game may collect certain information automatically to provide ads, process purchases, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.

3) Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and process information such as:

  • Device identifiers (for example, the Advertising ID)
  • IP address (often used to infer approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction and performance data (impressions, clicks)
  • Diagnostics and anti-fraud signals
Personalized vs. non-personalized ads: Where required by law (for example, in the EEA/UK), Google may request consent to serve personalized ads. If consent is not granted (or not required), Google may serve non-personalized ads where available.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4) In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by the platform store (for example, Google Play). We do not receive or store your full payment details (such as credit card numbers).

5) Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers to store personal data from the Game. Third-party services (like AdMob and the app store) may retain data according to their own policies and legal obligations.

6) Children and Age Eligibility

The Game is not directed specifically to children, but it is intended for a general audience (all ages may play). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information to us directly, contact us at dalenziidev@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

If you ever decide to target children as your primary audience, you should enable child-directed settings in your ad configuration.

7) Your Rights and Choices

EEA/UK (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may have rights under GDPR, including access, deletion, objection, and withdrawal of consent for certain processing.

When applicable, consent preferences for advertising are presented in the Game via Google’s consent flow (User Messaging Platform).

California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights to request information about data collected and to request deletion. Advertising-related processing is handled primarily by third parties (such as Google AdMob).

8) Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the Game and its integrations configured securely. No system is 100% secure, but we aim to minimize risk and use reputable providers.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page and reflected by the “Last Updated” date at the top.

10) Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: dalenziidev@gmail.com
